WHAT IS WORLDWATCH?

WorldWatch is a real-time global intelligence platform that aggregates breaking news, seismic events, military conflicts, cyberattacks and natural disasters onto an interactive world map. Our AI analyzes each event and assesses its potential impact on financial markets including Bitcoin (BTC), crude oil (WTI), gold, the S&P 500, Nasdaq and EUR/USD.

By correlating geopolitical events with market movements, WorldWatch helps traders, analysts and curious citizens understand why markets move and what global events are driving price action in real time.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

We pull data from multiple sources including GDELT (Global Database of Events, Language and Tone), USGS real-time earthquake feeds, and live aircraft transponder data. Our AI engine then classifies each event by type, location, severity and potential market impact — automatically highlighting critical events that could move oil prices, trigger gold rallies or cause equity volatility.
